Instructions

One card will be distributed over to every player facedown and another card faceup.
Aces count as 1 or 11, and face cards as 10. The rest of the cards are counted by their values.
The player goes first and must decide whether to "stand" (not ask for another card), "hit" (ask for another card in an attempt to get closer to a count of 21, or even hit 21 exactly), or go "bust" (if it is over 21)
When the dealer's total is 17 or more, it must stand. If the total is 16 or under, they must take a card.
Both can also win with 5 cards in hand without "bust"
